# Mythos Trusted Access & Data Retention

**Because safeguard-lifted [[mythos-class-models|Mythos-class]] capability is high-risk, Anthropic gates it behind trusted-access programs and a new 30-day data-retention policy rather than open release.**

**Mythos 5 deployment.** Mythos 5 — Fable 5 with cyber safeguards lifted — is initially deployed through **Project Glasswing**, in collaboration with the US government, as an upgrade to Claude Mythos Preview for cyber defenders and critical-infrastructure providers. Anthropic plans to steadily expand access in consultation with the US government and to open a **trusted access program** letting cybersecurity organizations apply systematically. A separate **biology trusted-access program** is planned, providing Fable 5 with the biology/chemistry safeguards removed (but cyber safeguards kept) to a small set of life-science researchers.

**Availability.** Fable 5 is available everywhere from launch; Mythos 5 is restricted to Glasswing partners (cyber safeguards lifted) and soon select biology researchers (bio/chem safeguards lifted). Subscription rollout is staged: included free on Pro/Max/Team/seat-based Enterprise through June 22, then requiring usage credits, with the intent to restore it as a standard plan feature once capacity allows.

**Data retention.** For Fable 5, Mythos 5, and future models of similar-or-higher capability, Anthropic requires **30-day retention for all traffic** on first- and third-party surfaces. The data is **not** used to train models or for any non-safety purpose; new privacy protections log all human access and ensure deletion after 30 days in almost all cases. Its purpose is defending against complex, novel, cross-request attacks (e.g. new jailbreaks) and reducing false positives from the [[fable-5-safety-classifiers]].
